## 🎯 Revenue Drivers (Post-Month 1)

**Focus these activities to drive conversions:**

1. **Content-to-Product Flow**
   - Blog post → Link to Features (3x)

2. **Email Capture**
   - Downloadable resources (checklists, templates)

   - Follow-up sequences: 5-7 emails nurturing to trial

3. **Social Proof**
   - Video testimonials from real garage users

4. **Urgency & CTAs**
   - "Start Free" CTA on every blog post

## 🛠️ Tools You'll Need

**Content Creation:**
- Grammarly (~$12/month) - spelling/grammar
- Hemingway Editor (free online) - readability
- Canva ($13/month or free) - graphics

**SEO Tracking:**
- Google Search Console (free) - essential
- Google Analytics 4 (free) - traffic tracking
- Ahrefs OR SEMrush (~$99/month) - keyword rankings, backlinks
- Screaming Frog (free up to 500 URLs) - site audits

**Outreach:**
- Hunter.io (free tier) - find email addresses
- BuzzSumo (free tier) - find shareable content
- Notion (free) - content calendar

## 🚨 What NOT To Do

❌ Don't create thin content (<1000 words)
❌ Don't publish without proofreading
❌ Don't buy backlinks (Google penalty)
❌ Don't keyword-stuff (ignore this: looks spammy)
❌ Don't ignore analytics (measure everything)
❌ Don't expect results in Week 1 (SEO takes 12+ weeks)
❌ Don't abandon after Month 1 if traffic is small
❌ Don't post "me-too" content (be unique)
